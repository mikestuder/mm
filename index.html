Traveling to the Wedding

We recommend booking flights directly to Florence (FLR) whenever possible—it’s the closest airport to the ceremony, rehearsal dinner, and our recommended hotels.

If you’re flying internationally, you have two options:

  • Fly directly into Florence (FLR) → Best option if available.
  • Fly into a major airport first (Rome or Milan), then connect to Florence:
    • From Rome (FCO) → High-speed train to Florence (~1 hr 30 mins).
    • From Milan (MXP) → Train to Florence (~2 hrs).

Where to Stay for the Wedding

We suggest staying in Florence for the ceremony and rehearsal dinner. We don’t have group bookings, but we highly recommend reserving early since June is peak season.
